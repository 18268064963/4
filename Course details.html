Computer Application Technology

Computer application technology is a junior college major in China's general institutions of higher learning, with a three-year schooling system. This major cultivates the all-round development of morality, intelligence, physique and beauty, has good professional ethics and humanistic quality, masters the knowledge of computer systems and information processing, and has the ability of database management, data processing, construction of big data application systems, and big data analysis and processing. High-quality technical and skilled personnel for information collection, processing and analysis in the information industry and business service industry. The courses that this major needs to learn include "Computer Fundamentals", "Computer System Assembly and Maintenance", "Website Planning and Production" and so on. The future career direction is network information enterprises and institutions: website design and development, network planning and design, network system design and testing, application software development, system management and maintenance, information product design and technical support.

1.Computer Fundamentals

2.Computer System Assembly and Maintenance

3.Website Planning and Production
